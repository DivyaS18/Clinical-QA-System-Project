The physician advised strict lifestyle modifications, including daily 30-minute brisk walking, weight loss through calorie control, and stress reduction through yoga or meditation. She was instructed to follow a low-glycemic index diet, avoid sugary drinks, consume more vegetables and whole grains, and reduce intake of saturated fats. Smoking and alcohol consumption were to be strictly avoided. Additionally, she was advised to monitor her blood glucose levels at home, maintain a log, and return for follow-up in 4 weeks to assess her response to therapy.
